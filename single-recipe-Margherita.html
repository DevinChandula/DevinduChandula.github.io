Margherita

Margherita pizza, a true Italian classic, is a testament to simplicity and exquisite taste. Named after Queen Margherita of Italy, this iconic pizza embodies the vibrant colors of the Italian flag with its combination of fresh tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, and basil leaves. The harmony of flavors is a culinary delight - the sweetness of sun-ripened tomatoes, t he creaminess of melted mozzarella, and the aromatic freshness of basil, all resting on a crispy yet chewy crust. This pizza celebrates the essence of Italian cuisine, showcasing how a few quality ingredients can create an unforgettable gastronomic experience. The Margherita pizza is not just a dish; it's a legacy, representing the rich heritage and love for authentic flavors that resonate from Naples to the world.

prep time

01 hr.

cook time

07 min.

servings

4 servings

Pizza Pasta Dinner Food

instructions

step 1

Start by preheating your oven to its highest temperature.

step 2

Roll out pizza dough, spread a layer of tomato sauce, add fresh slices of mozzarella cheese, and top it with fresh basil leaves.

step 3

Bake the pizza in the hot oven for around 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.

ingredients

One standard-sized pizza dough ball

1/2 to 3/4 cup (120-180 ml) of tomato sauce

Approx. 200-250 grams of fresh mozzarella cheese

tools

Baking Sheet

Pizza Peel

Rolling Pin

Pizza Cutter
