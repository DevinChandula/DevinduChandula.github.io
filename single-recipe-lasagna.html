Neapolitan Lasagna

Neapolitan Lasagna, an emblematic dish hailing from the vibrant city of Naples in Italy, is a culinary masterpiece that tantalizes taste buds with its rich flavors and comforting layers. Crafted with love and tradition, this lasagna is a symphony of tender pasta sheets, savory ragù, creamy béchamel sauce, and a generous sprinkling of Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese. What distinguishes Neapolitan Lasagna is its authenticity—slow-cooked ragù made from a blend of beef and pork, enriched with tomatoes and aromatic herbs. Each bite reveals a harmonious marriage of flavors, offering a glimpse into the heart of Italian cuisine and the warm embrace of Naples' culinary heritage.

prep time

45 min.

cook time

50 min.

servings

2 servings

Pizza Pasta Dinner Food

instructions

step 1

Begin by preparing a rich ragù sauce by gently simmering a blend of beef and pork with tomatoes, herbs, and spices until it thickens and flavors meld.

step 2

Layer cooked lasagna sheets with the ragù, creamy béchamel sauce, and a sprinkle of Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese in a baking dish.

step 3

Bake in the oven until the top turns golden brown, then allow it to rest before serving, allowing the flavors to intensify for a delightful Neapolitan Lasagna experience.

ingredients

Approx. 2-15 lasagna sheets

500 grams (1 pound) of ground beef

50 grams (3 1/2 tablespoons) of butter

tools

Dutch Oven

Baking Dish

Wooden Spoon

Strainer
