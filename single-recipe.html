Prosecco Negroni

The Prosecco Negroni is a delightful variation of the classic Negroni cocktail, renowned for its bittersweet and citrusy profile. This effervescent twist infuses the traditional Negroni ingredients—gin, Campari, and sweet vermouth—with the sparkling charm of Prosecco. The marriage of these elements creates a refreshing and lively drink that balances the bitterness of Campari with the effervescence of Prosecco, resulting in a pleasantly crisp and vibrant cocktail. The Prosecco Negroni offers a sophisticated yet approachable drinking experience, making it an ideal choice for those seeking a unique and effervescent take on the beloved Negroni cocktail.

instructions

step 1

combine 1 ounce each of gin, Campari, and sweet vermouth in a mixing glass with ice.

step 2

Stir well for 20-30 seconds, then strain the mixture into a chilled glass.

step 3

Top it off with Prosecco and garnish with an orange twist or slice for a vibrant and effervescent take

ingredients

1 ounce of gin

1 ounce of Campari

1 ounce of Sweet Vermouth
